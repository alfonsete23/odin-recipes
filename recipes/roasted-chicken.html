Roasted Chicken

Description

Roasted Chicken is a very simple dish made with chicken and potatoes

It usually contains some herbs like thyme or rosemary

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Potatoes
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes in stripes
  2. On an oven sheet, place the potatoes and the chicken on top along the herbs
  3. Cook it in the oven for 30 minutes or until crispy
