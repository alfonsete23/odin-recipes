Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a typical italian dish made with pasta and beef ragu.

It is usually topped with pecorino romano or parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Minced beef
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with the eggs to make the pasta
  2. Cook the beef with the tomato to make the beef ragu
  3. Assemble the lasagna in layers and top it with grated cheese
  4. Put it in the oven until the cheese is melted
